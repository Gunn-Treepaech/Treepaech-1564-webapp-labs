COVID-19

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.The statistics taken from Worldometers website fro the first top three that has the most number of cases are shown in section Statistics

Most common symptoms:

  • fever
  • dry cough
  • tiredness

Less common symptoms:

  1. aches and pains
  2. sore throat
  3. headache
  4. loss of taste or smell
